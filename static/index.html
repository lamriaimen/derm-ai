How to Use

1

Take a photo

Take a clear, well-lit photo of the skin lesion. Make sure the lesion is centered and in focus.

2

Upload the image

Drag and drop your image into the upload zone, or click to browse your files.

3

Click Analyze

After uploading, click the 'Analyze Lesion' button. Results appear within seconds.

4

Read the results

The most likely condition is shown with a confidence percentage, plus scores for all conditions.

Tips for better results

  • Use natural daylight ‚Äî avoid flash or fluorescent lighting
  • Keep the camera steady and close to the lesion (10-15 cm)
  • Make sure the lesion is centered in the image
  • Avoid images with hair, clothing or shadows covering the lesion
  • Works best with close-up photos of a single lesion
